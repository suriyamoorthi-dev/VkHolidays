Nourishment for Life

I am Reshmaa, Founder of Uyir Ootam. My journey began with a personal struggle against lactose intolerance, which ignited a lifelong passion for nutrient-dense, natural foods.

Inspired by my farming roots and the timeless principles of Nammalvar, I discovered the magic of microgreens. These tiny greens were not just food, but a powerful, gut-friendly solution to my nutritional needs.

Uyir Ootam was born from this discovery—a promise to deliver true nourishment for life, cultivated with care and organic integrity.
